Our Story

Tibetan Youth Initiative is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit led by high school students passionate about our heritage. We are dedicated to empowering Tibetan youth worldwide through education, cultural preservation, and community initiatives, fostering leadership and impact in diverse areas.

A Message From Our Founder

Nyida Gyal

"Our team of high schoolers is passionate about making a change, and doing so requires a sense of obligation. Significant change is not achieved all at once, but is accumulated through an abundance of time, people, and consistent efforts. For any high schoolers interested in becoming involved with the Tibetan Youth Initiative, feel free to email us at tibetanyouthinitiative@gmail.com, and be a part of making change from local communities to across the world."

Mewoen Tsuglag Petoen School

Mewoen Tsuglag Petoen School, founded in 2005 in Dharamsala, is a model school for Tibetan children in exile that combines modern education with traditional Tibetan language and values. Managed by the Sambhota Tibetan Schools Society, it teaches in Tibetan up to class III and introduces additional languages later, focusing on preserving Tibetan culture while providing quality education.
