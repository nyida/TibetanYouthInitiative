About Us

Tibetan Youth Initiative (TYI) is a youth-led nonprofit driven by the passion to uplift Tibetan communities through direct action, education, and cultural preservation. Founded by high school students, TYI blends modern advocacy with deep-rooted heritage, empowering a new generation of Tibetan leaders.
